Gissella Resuello

Digital Media Artist. Horror Noodles.

I'm a first year enrolled at San Jose State University studying for my BFA in the Digital Media Arts program. Having grown up with an artistic background inspired by the story-telling of animators and paintings of the digital artists of my time, I choose to explore and experiment with a variety of styles in my drawings and how variations in design can emphasize a movement or an action or convey an expression in a person, as it is to be able to tell stories with my drawings or capture a thought or feeling like a photograph.
